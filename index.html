I am Tengfei Cheng, a passionate and dedicated undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and Technology at Guangxi University, with a strong focus on research and practical applications. I thrive on exploring, learning, and creating solutions from scratch rather than relying on pre-existing frameworks. Under the guidance of Dr. Yunxuan Dong, I have authored a first-author paper currently under review. Additionally, I contributed to a provincial-level innovation project centered on visual gesture recognition.

My research interests lie at the intersection of innovative modeling, time series analysis, and large language models (LLMs). I am particularly fascinated by the application of artificial intelligence in scientific discovery, especially in designing AI models to address domain-specific challenges. I believe that time series data encapsulates latent semantic information (e.g., trends in power generation), and extracting this information effectively could bridge the gap between time series patterns and LLMs' language understanding capabilities. In essence, my core research focus is: Innovating and Applying Artificial Intelligence Models (Algorithm Design).

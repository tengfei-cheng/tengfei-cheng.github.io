I am Tengfei Cheng, a passionate and dedicated student majoring in Computer Science and Technology at Guangxi University, with a strong focus on research and practical applications. I prefer to explore, learn, and create things from scratch rather than simply relying on pre-made solutions provided by others. During my time at university, I participated in a research project supervised by Dr. Yunxuan Dong, where I authored a paper as the first author, which is currently under review. Additionally, I was involved in a university-level innovation and entrepreneurship project based on visual gesture recognition methods.
My research interests primarily focus on innovative modeling across multiple domains, including time series and large language models (LLMs). I am also deeply interested in the application of artificial intelligence in scientific inquiry, particularly in developing AI models tailored to address specific scientific problems. In my view, time series data contains hidden semantic information (e.g., whether power generation is increasing or decreasing). Therefore, exploring better methods for extracting semantic information holds the potential to enable more efficient interaction between the inherent patterns of time series and the language understanding capabilities of LLMs. To summarize, my fundamental research interest is: Innovating and Applying Artificial Intelligence Models (Designing Algorithms).
